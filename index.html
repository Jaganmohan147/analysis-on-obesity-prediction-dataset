Abstract

Obesity is a growing health problem worldwide. It is caused by factors such as diet, physical activity, genetics, and lifestyle choices. This project analyzes an Obesity Prediction Dataset to identify key factors influencing obesity. Using Pandas Profiling, we perform Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) to find patterns in the data. The goal is to understand how different habits and conditions contribute to obesity. 

Introduction

Obesity increases the risk of diseases like diabetes, heart disease, and hypertension. Many factors, including food habits, exercise levels, and sleep patterns, affect a person’s weight. By analyzing this dataset, we can identify which factors contribute most to obesity. This can help in spreading awareness and encouraging healthier lifestyles. 

Literature Survey

Several studies show that poor diet and lack of physical activity are major causes of obesity. Genetics also plays a role, but lifestyle changes can reduce obesity risks. Researchers use data analysis and machine learning to predict obesity levels based on habits and medical history. Pandas Profiling makes it easy to analyze large datasets and find key obesity trends. 

EDA & Project Overview

EDA helps in understanding the dataset and identifying patterns. Using Pandas Profiling, we analyze:

• Age and gender – How obesity varies across different groups.
• Diet habits – The impact of junk food and sugar consumption.
• Physical activity levels – The effect of exercise on obesity.
• Medical history – How pre-existing conditions influence weight gain.
• Correlations – Finding links between lifestyle choices and obesity levels.
